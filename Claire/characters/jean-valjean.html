Jean Valjean
Actor — Unaffiliated
Convict 24601. Stole bread. Served nineteen years. Broke parole. Was shown mercy by a bishop who gave him silver candlesticks and told him to become an honest man. He did. He became a mayor, a factory owner, a father. Then the law found him again because the law always does. He is the strongest man in any room and the gentlest, and the distance between those two things is where his whole life happens.
