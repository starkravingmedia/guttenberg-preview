World of Guttenberg

Style Guide

A visual language of parchment, ink, and intention. Everything here is meant to feel like it was printed before electricity, then somehow loaded in a browser.

✶ ✶ ✶

Usage

Link the stylesheet. Everything follows.

HTML
<link rel="stylesheet" href="styles/guttenberg.css">

The stylesheet imports Google Fonts (UnifrakturCook, Cinzel, EB Garamond) automatically via @import. No additional font links needed.

Typography

Three faces. Each with a purpose.

UnifrakturCook
Display only. Page titles. One weight (700). Use sparingly.
Cinzel Bold
Cinzel Regular
Section headers, card titles, badges, team names, numbers. The workhorse serif. Weights 400 and 700.
EB Garamond — the body speaks here, in sentences that breathe.
EB Garamond Italic — for the quieter voice, the aside, the thought.
EB Garamond SemiBold — emphasis within body text.
Body text, descriptions, subtitles, epigraphs, notes. Base size: 18px, line-height: 1.6.
Hierarchy
Main Title    UnifrakturCook 700   3em      .main-title
Subtitle      Cinzel 400           0.95em   .subtitle      uppercase, letter-spaced
Section Head  Cinzel 700           1.5em    .section-header uppercase, letter-spaced
Section Sub   EB Garamond italic   0.95em   .section-sub
Card Title    Cinzel 700           1.05em   .card-title
Card Desc     EB Garamond 400      0.95em   .card-desc
Body          EB Garamond 400      18px     body

Page Structure

Every page is parchment floating in ink

HTML
<body>
<div class="page-wrapper">
  <div class="parchment-bg">

    <h1 class="main-title">Page Title</h1>
    <p class="subtitle">A Subtitle in Small Caps</p>

    <!-- content here -->

    <p class="colophon">Footer text, italicized.</p>

  </div>
</div>
</body>

The body background is --ink (near-black brown). The .parchment-bg provides the textured cream surface with a double-border frame (outer 2px solid + inner 1px via ::before). Use .page-wrapper--wide for 1000px max-width instead of 900px.

Responsive Breakpoints

Three thresholds, graceful reduction

768px
Parchment padding reduces. Title drops to 2em. Diagram nodes and labels shrink. Legend tightens.
640px
Cards stack vertically (flex-direction: column). Card images go full-width at 160px height.
480px
Title drops to 1.6em. Subtitle letter-spacing tightens. Chain steps and legend compress. Padding minimizes.

The design degrades gracefully. Nothing breaks — it just gets quieter.

Skill Block

Roll skills with trigger, mechanic, ranges, and cost

Devastating Observation Active
Trigger: Sei chooses to study a person or situation closely and deliver her assessment
d20 + CHA (+3)
18+ She identifies not just what someone is hiding, but WHY — the emotional architecture beneath the mask.
10–17 She reads the surface accurately — posture, tells, inconsistencies.
